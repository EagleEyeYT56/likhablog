Likha Studios Partnered Checon Industries: Boosting Their Online Presence!

Gab

Hi there!

We have some exciting news to share with you! Likha Studios, the digital marketing team from Canada, has partnered with Checon Industries, a family-owned business based in the Philippines that specializes in fire safety equipment such as fire extinguishers and fire alarms. They've been serving their customers since 1993 and are committed to providing top-notch products and services.

We're working with Checon Industries to revamp their website, emails, and social media pages to create a standout online presence. Our team is dedicated to designing modern and engaging content that showcases their fantastic products and services. We're also keeping their valued customers informed with regular updates through email and social media.

We're truly excited about this partnership and the opportunity to help Checon Industries thrive online. Stay tuned for some exciting updates and announcements from the newly-formed partnership between Likha Studios and Checon Industries!

Thank you for your support as we continue to grow and make a positive impact online!
